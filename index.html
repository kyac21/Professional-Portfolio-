About Me

I'm Kya D. Carroll — an applied sports scientist and Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (CSCS) with a Master of Science in Sports Science (4.0 GPA) from the University of Pittsburgh and a Bachelor of Arts in Health Sciences (with distinction) from Hendrix College. I'm currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Kinesiology.

My work lives at the intersection of data and decision-making. I design and manage athlete monitoring systems, administer physical performance assessments, and build the reporting infrastructure that turns raw numbers into daily coaching decisions — across professional basketball, U.S. Olympic programs, and Division I collegiate sport.

I'm a published researcher in physiology, with graduate-level training spanning exercise physiology, biomechanics, neuromuscular physiology, and rehabilitation sciences. I believe that great sports science is equal parts rigorous analysis and clear communication — the data only matters if the people who need it can use it.
